Frequently Asked Questions



What is BiteToEat all about?

      BiteToEat is a food service company which provides customers with endless food services at their doorstep.



How do I setup my store on BiteToEat?

      In order to setup a store, please fill up the registration form on this website. We will contact you within 24 hours and help you setting up your store in less than a week.



What documents are required to do business with BiteToEat?

      You are required to have the following documents :

  • FSSAI Certificate
  • Food License
  • Bank Account
  • Pancard
  • KYC Documents



Will I be charged on setting up my store?

      No, you won't be charged anything for setting up the store on BiteToEat. Its totally a free process.



How do I list my products on BiteToEat?

      As soon as you set up your store on BiteToEat, you can start listing your products. You will also be given access to online panels through which you can easily manage your account.



What if my order gets cancelled?

      If an order is cancelled by the BiteToEat or customer, we will refund the full order amount back within 24 hours.



Can I close my store?

      Yes, you are free to close your store anytime.



When will I get paid and how?

      BiteToEat will make payment directly to your bank account through NEFT transaction within 7 days after deducting our commision.



Does BiteToEat provide packaging material?

      We provide you with the best packaging material which ensures that your product remains safe and undamaged.



Who do I contact to for more information?

      You can email us at info@BiteToEat.com
